All Our Services

Pool Design

PuraVida uses the latest technology when it comes to pool design. We use 3D rendering to show you what your pool will look like before we even start. We also use the latest technology to make sure your pool is built to last.

Pool Renovations

Time for a renovation? Updating your pool and coping can change the look of your pool. We can also add a spa, water features, and more. We can also add a new liner to your pool.

Openings & Closings

Spring coming to the Jersey Shore? Let our experts get your pool ready for the season with our full opening, which always includes an initial cleaning.

Pool Maintenance

Sit back and relax and let PuraVida do the rest. Our full maintenance plan includes opening, weekly cleanings, equipment maintenance and closing.
