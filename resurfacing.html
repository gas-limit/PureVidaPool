THE PROBLEM WITH NOT COMPLETING POOL RESURFACING INSTALLATION


1. Decreased Pool Longevity: Without proper resurfacing, your pool's structure can degrade faster, leading to costly repairs or even the need for a complete rebuild.

2. Safety Hazards: Cracks and rough surfaces not only look unappealing but can also be a safety hazard, increasing the risk of cuts and injuries.

3. Lowered Aesthetic Appeal: An unsurfaced pool can quickly lose its visual appeal, diminishing the overall look and feel of your outdoor space. Increased Maintenance Costs: Pools with deteriorating surfaces often require more chemicals and cleaning, leading to higher maintenance costs. Reduced Property Value: A well-maintained pool can be an asset, but a pool in poor condition can negatively impact your property's value.
